Carbonara

Cheesy, rich and silky smooth Carbonara pasta

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti - 100g
  • Bacon - 80g
  • Egg - 1
  • Parmesan - 50g
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta for about 12 minutes or until al dente.
  2. Cut the bacon into cubes and cook until crispy.
  3. Crack an egg into a bowl and wisk it. Now, add grated parmesan, pepper and mix.
  4. Strain the pasta (remember to keep some of the pasta water) and add it to the bacon

    together with the egg mixture and mix vigurously adding some pasta water if needed.

Plate and enjoy!
